# PHASE 1 — PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

# Project Name

**GativCare MedLingo AI**

**Tagline:**
*"The World's Most Advanced AI Medical Communication & Interpretation Platform."*

---

# Vision

Build the world's first enterprise-grade AI Medical Communication Intelligence Platform that enables seamless communication between patients, doctors, nurses, hospitals, coordinators, caregivers, and international medical tourism providers regardless of language.

This platform is not a translator.

It is an AI-powered Medical Communication Intelligence System that combines multilingual communication, clinical understanding, contextual interpretation, healthcare coordination, and patient assistance into one intelligent platform.

The AI should communicate like a highly experienced multilingual physician and certified medical interpreter while always supporting—not replacing—the judgment of licensed healthcare professionals.

The platform should be suitable for:

* International Hospitals
* Medical Tourism Companies
* Government Healthcare Organizations
* Private Hospitals
* Clinics
* Telemedicine Providers
* Diagnostic Centers
* Insurance Companies
* Emergency Medical Services
* Pharmaceutical Organizations
* Rehabilitation Centers

The product must be designed as an enterprise SaaS platform with multi-tenant architecture so each organization has complete isolation of its own users, branding, hospitals, doctors, data, settings, and permissions.

The AI should provide the previously shared information without asking the patient to repeat it, subject to user consent and organizational privacy settings.
